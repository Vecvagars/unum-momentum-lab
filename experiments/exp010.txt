Art direction:
Create an immersive painterly-surreal world around the central symbol or emotion.
The image should contain foreground, middle ground and background.
Use rich but uplifting color fields, soft atmospheric clouds, flowing forms, ornamental geometry, symbolic details, particles, architectural fragments and emotional landscape.
The central symbol should be recognisable but transformed into art.
The world must feel alive, layered, cinematic and suitable for projection mapping on a large facade.

Emotional direction:
Prioritise wonder, hope, warmth, tenderness, curiosity and calm.
The image must feel inviting, magical and uplifting.
Mystery is allowed only as gentle poetic mystery.
Avoid fear, threat, horror, violence, grimness, dark fantasy, occult symbolism, apocalyptic mood, evil atmosphere, creepy silhouettes, ritual sacrifice, monsters and menacing expressions.

Avoid:
- plain black background with a single icon
- flat logo/poster composition
- photorealism
- ordinary literal illustration
- text, letters, logos, UI, borders
- realistic people or faces
- horror, dark fantasy, evil, occult, threatening or grim atmosphere

Final goal:
A rich symbolic world where the visitor recognises the essence of their input, but the result feels like a poetic, immersive, positive artwork.
